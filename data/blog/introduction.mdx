# Introduction 

Hey everyone! My name is Mike, er, actually it's Jacob. I'm Mike's grandson. My Grandpa has been the proud owner of MikeLakies.com for ~20 years now, and he bought my domain (JacobLakies.com) back in like 2005. Recently there was a messup with GoDaddy and my Grandpa's original site data was lost. So I've decided to help upgrade the site and make it better than ever. 

Let's start out here by introducing a few things that my Grandpa loves (it is his site after all).
